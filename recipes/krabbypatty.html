Krabby Patty

Tasy and Easy Guacamole

The Krabby Patty! SpongeBob's masterpiece with a secret so hard to break that could drive the tiniest of organisms crazy. Here you get to recreate and enjoy this hall of fame burger in all it's glory. Roll up your sleeves, put on your squarest pants and let's get to work!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chuck steak
  • 1 pound bone-in short ribs
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp MSG
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 ounces shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 small vidalia onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 beefsteak tomato, sliced thinly
  • Ketchup to taste
  • Mustard to taste
  • 1 large dill pickle, sliced thinly
  • 4 sesame seed hamburger buns

Directions fo 4 third-pound burgers

  1. Trim and cut beef into 1-inch pieces, and spread evenly on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Place in the freezer for 15 minutes, along with the blade of a food processor, until beef is firm.
  3. Pulse in the food processor until beef is ground and pebbly. Shape into 4 patties, and season with salt, pepper, and MSG.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat until barely smoking. Add patties and reduce heat to medium - cook to desired doneness.
  5. On the burger buns, assemble the burgers in this order: patty, lettuce, cheese, onion, tomato, ketchup, mustard, pickles, top bun.

And there you have it, you own homemade Krabby Patties!
